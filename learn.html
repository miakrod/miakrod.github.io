Meet the family!

Penny Juice is a family-owned business founded in 2001. We have over 40 years of experience in the beverage industry. Ma and Pa built the foundation of Penny Juice and we, their children, continue their legacy.

Premium Quality, 100% Juice Concentrate

Most ingredients in our juice concentrates are grown right here at the Penny Family Farm. We guarantee each sip and slurp is a delicious source of Vitamin C without any added sugars or artifical flavors.
Ingredients include: Concentrated Juice, Filtered Water, Malic Acid, Ascorbic Acid (Vitamin C), and Sodium Benzonate to maintain freshness.
